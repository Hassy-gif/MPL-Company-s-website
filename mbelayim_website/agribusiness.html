Mbelayim Agribusiness: The Future of Goat Farming

Welcome to the Mbelayim Pvt Goat Farm and Breeding Centre, a state-of-the-art, 2-acre (4-hectare) facility located in Golinga, Tolon District, just 10km south of Tamale Metropolis.

Our Products & Services

Our farm is a comprehensive agricultural hub designed to support the entire value chain.

  • Sales of Processed Goat Meat
  • Dairy Production
  • Breeding Services
  • Livestock Sales
  • Farmer Support & Health Services
  • Research & Training Center

Our Breeds & Care

We have carefully selected breeds that are hardy and productive. Our stock of 50 female and 6 male goats includes:

  • African Creole
  • Anglo-Nubian

Our farm is equipped with modern facilities, including proper roads, electricity, a community dam, and a managed drainage system to ensure the health and peak performance of our animals.
