Mbelayim Construction is a key player in the development of regional infrastructure. We possess the equipment, expertise, and dedication to execute vital civil works projects that connect communities and drive economic activity.

Active Tenders & Projects

We are actively engaged in improving the infrastructure of the Savanna Region. As of June 2025, we are bidding for the reshaping of 8 lots of crucial gravel roads:

  • Buipe-Kotito No. 3 Road (km 0.0 - 60.0)
  • Sawla-Gbenyiri-Kalba Road (km 0.0 - 28.0)
  • Tuna-Soma-Jang Road (km 0.0 - 27.0)

Our proactive participation in these tenders shows our commitment to being a leading construction firm in the region.
