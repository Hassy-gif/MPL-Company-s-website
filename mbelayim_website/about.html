Our Story, Our Vision

Our Vision Statement

To become a leading, diversified company in the livestock farming and construction industries throughout the Northern Region and a premier exporter of Ghanaian products. We aim to be among the most respected brands in the country before our 10th anniversary.

Our Mission Statement

Our mission is to provide quality products and services to all our customers, wherever they may be. We are committed to supplying commercial quantities of processed goat meat and byproducts across the Northern regions and Ghana at large, while simultaneously delivering excellence in our construction and engineering projects.

Our Leadership & Foundation

Mbelayim Pvt Limited was founded by a team of passionate entrepreneurs: Hudeen, Wadud, and Kasuli. The company is steered by our Managing Director, Wadud Abdul Rahaman Ca., who brings strategic oversight and industry experience to all our ventures.

Our strength is built on a well-founded business structure of hardworking, honest, and skilled professionals. We foster healthy relationships with key players in both the agriculture and construction sectors, from suppliers and buyers to industry merchants, ensuring a powerful and effective network.
